What do you do after you donate?

Send us an email with the attached receipt of the donation, as a thank you we will send you all the codes for the in-game cosmetic shop and more that are not obtainable through playing the game. Donating would mean so much to us and allow us to continue to develop more games in the future

What should you do with the letter?

The linked letter in the menu of the homepage is for you to read and share with your parents. This is our follow up message for you upon completing the game, it contains some information that will explain how you can help out the environment in your own community and help spread the word about climate change.
